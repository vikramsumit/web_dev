Colors can be used to convey meaning, such as using red for errors or green for success.

Experiment with different colors to find the best combination for your design.

Use tools like color contrast checkers to ensure your color choices are accessible to all users.

Colors can also be used to create a mood or theme for your website, so choose wisely!
